GENERAL INFORMATION & INVOICES.

Packages arrive in Jamaica on Tuesdays and Saturdays; the shipments are closed off at 4pm on Saturdays and Wednesdays, respectively.ALL Order Invoices must be sent to airmillservicesja@gmail.com for package clearance at Jamaica Customs Agency. If you do not submit an invoice your packaged will be inspected in order to determine the Order Total and the Customs Duty amount to be charged.

FREIGHT & CUSTOMS CHARGES.

A Freight Charge is the cost to transport your packages from USA to Jamaica. It is as follows; $5.00 US or $700.00 Ja. for the first pound and $3.00 US or $400.00 Ja. for each additional pound.A Customs Duty Charge will apply on all orders exceeding USD$50.00 in total. Learn more about Customs Duty Charge at Jamaica Customs.

STORAGE FEES & ABONDONED PACKAGES

Packages left uncollected for over THREE (3) WEEKS from date of package arrival will attract a storage fee of JMD$100 per day. Storage fees must be paid upon collecting package(s).Packages left uncollected for over ONE (1) MONTH from the date of package arrival will be considered “Abandoned” and be disposed of.
